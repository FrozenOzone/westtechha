Flagship Panel
Garage Automation Panel (GAP)
Available • Veteran-built smart garage controller

A safety-conscious, Ethernet-first garage controller that combines door reeds, obstruction beam, motion sensors, and buzzer warnings with advanced auto-close logic and Home Assistant integration.

  • ESP32 + LAN8720 Ethernet with Wi-Fi backup
  • Door reeds, safety beam, inside/outside PIR inputs
  • Local logic with configurable auto-close & buzzer pre-warn
  • Front-facing LCD or OLED status display (per model)
