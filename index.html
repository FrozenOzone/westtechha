Built for DIYers & Installers
Snapshot

WestTechHA panels are designed for people who care how things are wired and labeled, not just how they look in an app. Every enclosure, GPIO map, and ESPHome baseline is created with future you—or a future installer—in mind.

  • Stable-tested ESPHome firmware, versioned like software releases
  • Clear GPIO maps and wiring guidance
  • Ethernet-first reliability with Wi-Fi as backup
  • Local logic for safety-critical behavior

As the product line grows, WestTechHA aims to provide access to firmware images, wiring guides, and 3D enclosure models so that systems remain serviceable and understandable for years.
